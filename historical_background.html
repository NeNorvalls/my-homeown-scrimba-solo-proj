Historical Background of San Joaquin, Iloilo

Colonial Era

San Joaquin was established during the Spanish colonial period and is known for its historical landmarks, such as the San Joaquin Church, which was built in 1869 and features a unique bas-relief of the Battle of Tetuan.

Cultural Heritage

The town's rich cultural heritage is celebrated through various festivals, such as the Pasungay Festival, which showcases traditional bullfighting and local customs.

Modern Development

Today, San Joaquin continues to blend its historical roots with modern development, attracting visitors with its scenic views, historical sites, and vibrant community life.
